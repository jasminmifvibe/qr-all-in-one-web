QR All-in-One

Effective date: [Month Day, Year]

QR All-in-One is an iOS app that allows users to scan QR codes using the device camera or images from the photo library, and to generate QR codes from text, links, and similar content.

Information and permissions

Camera

The app may request access to your device camera so you can scan QR codes in real time. Camera access is only used for the scanning feature.

Photo Library

The app may request access to your photo library so you can choose images to scan for QR codes, and so you can save generated QR codes to your device when supported.

Third-party services

Firebase Remote Config

QR All-in-One may use Firebase Remote Config to update certain app behavior or configuration values without requiring a full app update.

Google Mobile Ads / AdMob

QR All-in-One may display ads using Google Mobile Ads, also known as AdMob. These services may collect or process device and usage information as described in their own policies in order to provide and measure ads.

Because the app may show ads, some information may be processed by advertising or analytics-related services used by those providers.

Data collection

The developer does not intentionally collect unnecessary personal data directly through QR All-in-One. The app is designed primarily to perform QR code scanning and QR code generation on the device.

However, third-party services integrated into the app, such as Firebase Remote Config or Google Mobile Ads / AdMob, may process limited technical or device-related information as part of their services.

Your choices

You can control certain permissions, such as camera and photo library access, through your iPhone settings. If you deny these permissions, some app features may not work as intended.

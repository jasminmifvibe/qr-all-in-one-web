QR All-in-One

Scan QR codes from your camera or photos, and generate QR codes from text and links in one simple app.

Support Privacy Policy

What the app does

QR All-in-One helps you quickly scan QR codes using your iPhone camera or images from your photo library. You can also create your own QR codes from text, website links, and other supported content.
